Banana Pancake

- Menu -

  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of Milk
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • Oil

☕ Method ☕

  1. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients (baking powder and flour).
  2. In another bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, and brown sugar until the sugar has completely dissolved.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix well.
  4. Heat a frying pan with a bit of oil over medium heat.
  5. Spoon 2 to 3 tablespoons of the batter onto the hot pan to make a small circle.
  6. Cook until small bubbles form around the edges and the top is slightly dry.
  7. Flip and cook the other side until it turns a light golden brown.
  8. Slice the bananas into small circles.
  9. Layer a few slices of banana on each pancake.
  10. If desired, drizzle with chocolate syrup or pour chocolate over the top.
  11. Serve with a glass of milk or fruit juice.
  12. Enjoy!
